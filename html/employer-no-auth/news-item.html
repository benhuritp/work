Назва підрозділу

Anchored Euro is a euro-backed stablecoin created by Swiss issuer, Anchored Coins. Founded in 2022, Anchored Coins aims to achieve stability in the crypto space while fostering regulatory clarity and industry-wide compliance.

Anchored Euro is designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the euro. This means that, by design, 1 unit of should always be worth 1 euro. It combines the benefits of the digital and traditional worlds, providing users with a stable option in the crypto space.

Назва підрозділу

Let's delve into the potential benefits underlining the importance of in the evolving digital economy:

  1. Diversification: Although there are several euro-backed stablecoins, the cryptocurrency market is primarily dominated by USD-backed stablecoins such as USDT or USDC. This allows investors and traders to hedge against potential economic disruptions in the US or currency fluctuations between the USD and EUR.
  2. Cross-border transactions: AEUR can simplify cross-border transactions within Europe, making them quicker, cheaper, and more efficient than traditional systems.
  3. Compliance: Creating a euro-backed stablecoin compliant with European laws could be an important step in legitimizing cryptocurrency and blockchain technology usage within Europe.
  4. Interoperability: With AEUR, users have a digital counterpart to the euro, which can be used seamlessly for various transactions from buying goods to paying for services or transferring funds. Currently, AEUR exists on Ethereum and BNB Chain, with plans to extend its presence to other blockchains in the future.
